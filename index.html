About Me

I am a flower from Hawaii.

In a few Pacific Islands, such as Hawaii, Tahiti, Fiji, Samoa, New Zealand and the Cook Isands I am used for making leis.

In modern Polynesian culture, my flowers can be worn by women to indicate their relationship status -- over the right ear if seeking a relationship, and over the left if taken.
